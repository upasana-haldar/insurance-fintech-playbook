# Ecosystem Overview

## 1. What Insurance Actually Is

Insurance is a **risk transfer mechanism**.

Individuals or businesses pay a **premium**.

The insurer promises **financial protection if a specific event (risk) occurs**.

The insurer pools money from many customers to pay claims for the few who face losses.

Example:
If 10,000 people pay ₹5,000 premium for health insurance, the insurer uses that pooled money to pay hospital claims for those who get sick.

Insurance works like a risk transfer ecosystem, not just a company selling policies.

## 2. Main Types of Insurance
1. **Life Insurance** - Covers risk of death or long-term life goals. **Examples** : Term Insurance, Whole Life, Endowment Plans, ULIP (Unit Linked Insurance Plan)
2. **General (Non-Life) Insurance** - Covers assets or health. **Examples** : Health Insurance, Motor Insurance, Travel Insurance, Property Insurance, Marine Insurance, Liability Insurance

## 3. The ecosystem connects:

**1. Capital (Reinsurers) → 2. Risk Carriers (Insurers/ Insurance Companies) → 3. Distribution(Agents / Brokers / Banks / Aggregators / Embedded) → 4. Customers → 5. Service Networks (Claims & Service Ecosystem - Hospitals / Garages / Surveyors / TPAs) → 6. Technology Platforms**
